HISTORY

May's House shop is a clothing apparel family business, which is run by owner,Nhung Nguyen.

The first store was opened in 2001. At early stage, the core product is hand-made bag which is target to teenages. In early 2013, the store extend business line to variety of products such as t-shirt.

Now, May's House shop is a destinated shopping location of many teenages. The store just opened a new location in Ben Tre province to serve the demand of young customers.
